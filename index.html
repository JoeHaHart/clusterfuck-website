London

The last tuesday of every month The Miller is host to Clusterf**k, the anarchic comedy show curated by Best Boy and Total Comedy. Regularly compered by Joe Hart.

Clusterf**k is free entry with a donation bucket at the end which we split between all the acts.

We book comics we love. People who are doing bold, interesting funny things. Previous acts include: Kieran Hodgson, Story Beast, Glenn Moore, Surname and Surname, Goose, Jollyboat and Felicity Ward.
